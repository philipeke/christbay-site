What is ChristBay?

A quiet companion for your daily walk with God


ChristBay is a private devotional app for Christians who want a disciplined, intentional daily practice rooted in scripture. Each morning, open a curated Bible verse, work through a guided reflection, and spend a few minutes in the prayer journal — all in a clean, ad-free environment.


Grounded in Protestant conviction — the sufficiency of Scripture, grace through faith, and the call to daily sanctification — ChristBay is not a social platform. It is a personal tool for those who take their daily walk with God seriously.

Features

Built for your daily walk with God


Everything you need for a consistent, meaningful devotional life — nothing you don’t.

Morning Scripture

Begin each day anchored in God’s Word. A curated daily verse with brief context to ground your morning in scripture.

Guided Reflections

Thoughtful, scripture-based prompts that help you meditate on the Word and apply it honestly to your daily life.

Prayer Journal

A private space to record your prayers, praises, and requests — and to look back on God’s faithfulness over time.

Reflections Packs

Themed devotional series for deeper study — the Psalms, Paul’s letters, the Sermon on the Mount, and more.

Faith Journey

Track your daily streak, completed reflections, and growth milestones. A simple, honest record of a growing habit.

No Ads. Ever.

ChristBay is completely ad-free and never sells your data. Your faith practice stays between you and God.
