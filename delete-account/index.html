Your Privacy Rights

ChristBay is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Swedish Data Protection Act (2018:218), and international privacy standards. You have the right to request deletion of your account and personal data at any time.

This right is guaranteed under GDPR Article 17 (Right to Erasure), also known as the "Right to be Forgotten". OakDev & AI AB, as your data controller, is legally bound to fulfil verified deletion requests promptly and completely — subject only to limited, legally required retention obligations. Read our full Privacy Policy for complete details.

What to Expect

Deletion Timeline

Here is exactly what happens after you submit a deletion request, and when.

Immediately

Local data deleted

If you used the in-app deletion flow, all data stored locally on your device — your prayer journal, reflection history, Reflection balance, bookmarks, and settings — is permanently erased from your phone at this moment.

Within 5 business days

Acknowledgement & identity verification

For email requests, we will acknowledge receipt of your request and, where necessary, ask you to verify your identity (e.g., by confirming your Apple ID email or a transaction ID). This step protects your data from unauthorised deletion by a third party.

Within 30 days

Server-side deletion complete

All personal data held in our active systems is permanently deleted: your account identifier, authentication data (including Sign-In tokens), server-side Reflection balance, Blessed subscription flags, push notification token, and support correspondence. You will receive a confirmation email when this is complete.

Within 60 days

Backup purge complete

Encrypted system backup copies (retained for disaster recovery) are automatically rotated on a 30-day cycle. Your data will be fully purged from all backup systems within 60 days of your deletion request, leaving no trace in any of our infrastructure.

Ongoing for up to 7 years

Transaction records retained (legally required only)

Purchase transaction records (Apple receipt tokens, transaction IDs, product IDs, purchase dates) are retained in a restricted, access-controlled environment for 7 years from the date of purchase, as required by the Swedish Accounting Act. These records are not used for any purpose other than legal compliance, and access is limited to legally authorised personnel only.

GDPR Rights

All of Your Privacy Rights

Deletion is just one of the rights you have as a data subject under the GDPR. OakDev & AI AB is committed to honouring all of your data protection rights.

Right of Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We will provide this within 30 days.

Right to Rectification

You can ask us to correct inaccurate personal data we hold about you at any time.

Right to Erasure

The right described on this page — to have your personal data permanently deleted from our systems.

Right to Restriction

You can ask us to pause processing of your data in certain circumstances while a dispute is resolved.

Right to Portability

You can request a machine-readable export of the personal data we hold about you in our systems.

Right to Object

You can object to processing of your data based on legitimate interests at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@oakdev.app or read our full Privacy Policy for complete details on each right.
